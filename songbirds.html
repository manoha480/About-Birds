Song Birds

Song Birds are birds known for their beautiful and melodious singing. They have special vocal organs that allow them to produce a wide range of sounds. Common songbirds include nightingales, robins, and canaries. They are often found in gardens and forests, and their songs can be heard during the day and at dawn or dusk.
