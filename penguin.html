PENGUIN

PENGUINS are flightless birds primarily found in the Southern Hemisphere, especially in Antarctica. They are known for their distinctive black and white plumage and their upright posture. Penguins are excellent swimmers and spend a significant amount of time in the water.

These birds primarily feed on fish, squid, and krill. Penguins are social animals and often live in large colonies called rookeries.
