LOVE BIRDS

Love Birds are small, affectionate parrots known for their strong pair bonds. They are native to Africa and come in various colors, including green, yellow, and peach.

These birds are social and thrive in pairs or small groups. They are herbivores, primarily feeding on seeds, fruits, and vegetables. Love Birds are known for their playful nature and can be very affectionate with their owners.

            
Types of Love Birds

            
    
                
  • Peach-faced Love Bird - The most common type, known for its peach-colored face and green body.
    • 
                
  • Abyssinian Love Bird - Features a more vibrant coloration with a distinctive yellow face.
    • 
                
  • Fischer's Love Bird - Known for its bright green body and orange-yellow face.
    • 
                
  • Lilac-crowned Love Bird - A rarer type with a lilac crown and green body.
