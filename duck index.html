DUCK

DUCKSare medium-sized water birds that belong to the Anatidae family. They are known for their broad flat beaks, short necks, and webbed feet which help them swim. Ducks live in both freshwater and coastal areas, such as ponds, lakes, rivers, and marshes.

They are omnivores, eating plants, insects, small fish, and other aquatic organisms. Ducks are found all over the world and are both wild and domesticated.

Types of Ducks


    
    
  • Wood Duck - Known for its striking plumage and is often found in wooded swamps.

  • Mallard - The most common duck species, easily recognized by its green head.

  • Pekin Duck - A domesticated breed known for its meat and egg production.

  • Mandarin Duck - Famous for its colorful feathers and is native to East Asia.
