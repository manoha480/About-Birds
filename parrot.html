PARROT

Parrots are colorful, intelligent birds found mainly in tropical and subtropical regions. They belong to the bird order Psittaciformes, and are known for their strong curved beaks, bright feathers, and ability to mimic sounds or human speech.

Most parrots live in trees (arboreal) and feed on seeds, fruits, nuts, and buds.Parrots are social birds and are often kept as pets due to their friendly behavior and ability to learn tricks.

Types of Parrots


Macaw

    Large and colorful

    Native to Central and South America

    Very intelligent and playful

African Grey Parrot

    Famous for talking ability

    Grey body with a red tail

    One of the most intelligent bird species

Cockatoo

    Recognizable by their crest of feathers

    Mostly white or pink

    Native to Australia and nearby islands

Budgerigar (Budgie)

    Small and popular pet parrot

    Comes in green, blue, yellow colors

    Very social and easy to train

Lovebird

    Small, colorful, and loving nature

    Usually seen in pairs

    Native to Africa

Eclectus Parrot

    Males are green, females are red and blue

    Native to the Solomon Islands and nearby areas

    Calm and gentle in nature
