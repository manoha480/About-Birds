EAGEL

Eagle is a large and powerful bird of prey known for its sharp vision, strong talons, and hunting skills. It lives in many parts of the world and builds nests on high trees or cliffs.

Eagles eat fish, small animals, and birds. Famous types include the Bald Eagle, Golden Eagle, and Philippine Eagle. They are symbols of strength and freedom in many cultures.

Types of Eagle


Bald Eagle

    National bird of the USA.

    White head and tail with a brown body.

    Found in North America.

Golden Eagle

    Brown body with golden-brown feathers on the neck.

    One of the best-known birds of prey in the Northern Hemisphere.

Harpy Eagle

    Very large eagle found in rainforests of Central and South America.

    Has a crown of feathers and strong legs.
