BUDGIE

Budgies are small, colorful parrots native to Australia. They are known for their playful nature and ability to mimic sounds. Budgies have a variety of colors and patterns, making them popular pets worldwide.

These birds are social creatures that thrive in flocks. They are herbivores, primarily feeding on seeds, fruits, and vegetables. Budgies are also known for their intelligence and can learn tricks and words with proper training.

            
Types of Budgies

            
    
                
  • English Budgie - Larger and more docile than American budgies.
    • 
                
  • American Budgie - Smaller with a more active personality.
    • 
                
  • Australian Budgie - The wild type, known for its green and yellow plumage.
