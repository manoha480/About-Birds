SPARROW

Sparrows are small, brown or gray birds known for their cheerful chirping. They are friendly and often live near human homes.

Sparrows build nests in walls, roofs, or trees and feed on grains, seeds, and small insects. They are social birds and are usually seen in groups.

Types of Sparrows


House Sparrow

    Most common type

    Found near homes and cities

    Brown, black, and white feathers

Tree Sparrow

    Smaller than house sparrow

    Lives in open countryside and farms

    Has a black spot on its cheek

White-crowned Sparrow

    Black and white stripes on head

    Found in North America

    Beautiful singing voice

Song Sparrow

    Known for its melodious song

    Found near gardens, bushes, and streams

Chipping Sparrow

    Small with a rusty cap on the head

    Often seen in parks and open areas
